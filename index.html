About Pocketro

Pocketro is a fast-paced roguelike card game that fuses poker mechanics with strategic deck-building. You'll craft winning hands, collect random power-ups, and face tougher challenges with each round. Every decision shapes your run, making each session unpredictable and rewarding. Test your instincts, take risks, and outsmart the table in a game of clever gambits.
